# Product Overview

An AI-powered custom merchandise e-commerce platform that allows customers to create personalized products (t-shirts, mugs, caps, stickers) based on their mood or creative vision.

## Purpose

Enable customers to express themselves through custom merchandise by leveraging AI image generation. Customers input their mood via text prompts or upload images, which are processed through Amazon Bedrock to generate unique designs that are printed on their selected products.

## Key Features

### Customer-Facing Features
- **Mood-Based Design Generation**: Customers describe their mood through free-text prompts
- **Image Upload**: Customers can upload static images as design inspiration
- **AI Image Generation**: Integration with Amazon Bedrock for generating custom designs
- **Product Selection**: Choose from multiple product types (t-shirts, mugs, caps, stickers)
- **Design Preview**: View generated design on selected product before purchase
- **E-commerce Checkout**: Standard Shopify checkout experience

### Backend Features
- **Amazon Bedrock Integration**: AI image generation pipeline
- **Shopify Store**: Product catalog, cart, and order management
- **Shiprocket Integration**: Automated order fulfillment and shipping
- **Design Storage**: Manage generated designs and customer orders

## Business Model

Print-on-demand custom merchandise with AI-generated designs based on customer input.

## Target Audience

Customers seeking unique, personalized merchandise that reflects their mood, personality, or creative vision.
